Models gallery

CRV

With a turbocharged engine standard on non-hybrid trims, plus available AWD and a whole range of features to keep you connected and feeling at ease, the CR-V makes every drive a truly rewarding experience for its owner.

HRV

Versatility means having it all, including a stylish exterior. Combining an SUV stance with sporty details like redesigned wheels and an available honeycomb grille, the HR-V stands out everywhere—from the boulevard to the campground.

S2000

Honda’s two-seat, four-wheel sportbike offers rear-drive roadster fun in a spectacularly balanced chassis. A high-revving 237-hp four turns through a six-speed manual. The S2000CR is the hard-core track rat with a stiffer suspension and additional aero parts.

Accord

With a robust hybrid powertrain, power-flow monitor and EV mode, the 2021 Accord Hybrid offers everything that’s great about the much-loved Accord with the added benefit of increased fuel efficiency and higher performance.

Pilot

The 2021 Pilot is loaded with confidence-inspiring capabilities and an array of sophisticated features. From its now-standard 9-speed automatic transmission to a new Special Edition trim, this road-ready SUV is designed to help you take on any adventure.

Civic

The 2021 Civic Sedan features aggressive lines and refined features that make it stand out from the crowd. Signature C-shaped taillights, a sleek profile and a bold Sport trim add to its fierce personality and untamed soul.
